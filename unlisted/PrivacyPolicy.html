Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy covers PuzzleDrop and other simple, non-commercial mini-games and hobby projects developed by us and hosted statically via GitHub Pages.

These projects are built strictly for fun, personal learning, and portfolio display. They are completely free to use, run entirely in your web browser, and contain no ads or commercial monetization.

🔒 Zero Data Collection Policy We do not collect, store, track, or share any personal data, analytical metrics, or browser history. Because our projects are completely static, there is no server database or backend registering your information.

1. Local Integrations (e.g., Pinterest API)

Some of our visual projects, like PuzzleDrop, allow you to find or link public images (using tools like the Pinterest API) to convert them into interactive puzzles.

  • Browser-Only Operations: All API requests and image manipulations happen live in your browser window.
  • No Saving: We do not download, duplicate, or store your image URLs or search queries to any external host.
  • Third Parties: Interactions with third-party APIs are subject to their respective terms and privacy behaviors.

2. Cookies and Trackers

Our static game pages do not load tracking pixels, analytic cookies (such as Google Analytics), or behavioral trackers.

3. Static Hosting via GitHub

Our pages run on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log standard network requests (like IP addresses) solely to ensure hosting security and platform performance. This remains bound to the standard GitHub Privacy Statement.

4. Family-Safe

Because there is absolute zero data harvest or tracking, our simple projects are completely safe and private for players of all ages.
