How to play the game

🖼️

1. Upload Any Image or GIF

Drop a photo, paste an image link, or search for ANIMATED GIFs! 🌟 The puzzle adapts to your screen.

Drag pieces from the tray to the board. They will only snap and lock if placed in their correct spot!

Hold the "Hint" button to peek at the original image, or use "Reset Board" to clear pieces back to the tray.
